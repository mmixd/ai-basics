What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is a way for computers to do things that usually require human intelligence. This can include tasks like recognizing speech, understanding pictures, making decisions, or translating languages.

Key Takeaway: AI helps computers think and learn like humans, but they need a lot of data and practice to get better.
