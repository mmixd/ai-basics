How Does AI Work?

AI works by processing large amounts of data using algorithms. An algorithm is like a recipe that tells a computer what steps to follow to solve a problem or complete a task.

How AI Uses Algorithms: AI uses algorithms to learn from data. For example, if you show a computer thousands of pictures of cats, it can learn to recognize a cat in a new photo. This process is similar to how a person might learn to recognize different breeds of dogs by looking at many pictures over time.

Key Takeaway: AI needs data and instructions (algorithms) to learn and make decisions.
