Different Types of AI

Supervised Learning

In supervised learning, AI is given labeled data (like pictures of cats labeled 'cat') and learns to recognize new examples based on these labels.

Unsupervised Learning

In unsupervised learning, AI finds patterns on its own without any labels. It's like sorting a bag of mixed fruit into groups.

Reinforcement Learning

AI learns by trial and error, similar to how you learn to ride a bike. It gets rewards for doing the right thing and penalties for mistakes.

Key Takeaway: AI can learn in different ways, just like people do.
